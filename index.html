§ 04 The Beat
Four steps. The same ones every newsroom runs.

How a story — or a website — actually gets made.

Step 01

Brief

A short conversation and a written brief. What the business actually does, who it does it for, and where the current site is lying about it.

Step 02

Report

I interview you like a source. I dig through what customers say, what reviews say, and what the competition sounds like. The real story comes out here.

Step 03

Write

Every line of copy rewritten, every headline earned, every section structured to move a reader from landing to action. The site gets built around the writing, not the other way around.

Step 04

Ship

We publish. Launch checklist runs. Thirty days later we review what's working and what isn't, and we sharpen anything that's dull.
